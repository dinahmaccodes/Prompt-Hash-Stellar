## What is PromptHash Stellar?

PromptHash Stellar is a blockchain-powered marketplace for selling reusable AI prompts. Unlike traditional platforms, PromptHash gives you:

- **Full Control:** Your prompts are encrypted end-to-end; only buyers with verified wallet access can unlock them
- **Transparent Payments:** XLM transfers settle on-chain with zero intermediaries taking cuts
- **Repeat Revenue:** Each buyer gets permanent access; you can resell the same prompt infinitely
- **No Intermediary Risk:** Your earnings are controlled by smart contracts, not corporate policies

**In Plain English:**
- You write a prompt → You encrypt it → You set a price in XLM → Buyers pay → They get lifetime access

## Why Licensing, Not NFTs?

You might wonder: "Why not use NFTs?"

**The key difference:**

| Aspect | NFTs | PromptHash Licensing |
|--------|------|---------------------|
| **What buyer owns** | A unique token (collectible) | Permanent access rights |
| **Can creator resell?** | No (buyer owns it now) | Yes, infinite sales to different buyers |
| **Revenue model** | One-time sale per NFT | Recurring royalties per sale |
| **Buyer value** | Ownership/bragging rights | Actual usable content |
| **Best for** | Art, collectibles, uniqueness | Prompts, templates, knowledge products |

**Why PromptHash Uses Licensing:**
- You want to sell the same prompt to 100 people, not just one
- Buyers don't care about "owning" a prompt; they care about using it
- Licensing supports creator economy better than NFTs for knowledge products
- Transparent, simple revenue sharing on-chain

## Step 1: Set Up Your Freighter Wallet

### What is Freighter?

Freighter is a browser wallet extension that securely manages your Stellar account and XLM funds. It's like a digital bank account for cryptocurrency.

### Installation Instructions

1. **Go to Freighter Website:**
   - Visit https://www.freighter.app/
   - Click "Install Freighter" or go to your browser's extension store:
     - **Chrome:** Chrome Web Store, search "Freighter"
     - **Firefox:** Firefox Add-ons, search "Freighter"
     - **Brave:** Built-in extension store, search "Freighter"

2. **Install the Extension:**
   - Click "Add to Chrome" / "Add to Firefox" / etc.
   - Confirm the permission popup
   - Freighter icon should appear in your browser's top-right corner

3. **Create or Import Wallet:**

   **Option A: Create New Wallet**
   - Click Freighter icon
   - Click "Create Account"
   - Choose a strong password (minimum 12 characters, mix of uppercase, numbers, special characters)
   - **IMPORTANT:** Freighter will show your "Recovery Phrase" (12-24 words)
     - **SAVE THIS PHRASE** in a secure location (not on your computer!)
     - Do NOT share this phrase with anyone
     - You'll need it to restore your wallet
   - Click "I Saved My Recovery Phrase"
   - Confirm by re-entering some words
   - Done! Your wallet is created

   **Option B: Restore Existing Wallet**
   - Click Freighter icon
   - Click "Restore Account"
   - Enter your 12 or 24-word recovery phrase
   - Create a strong password
   - Done!

4. **Verify Your Account:**
   - Open Freighter
   - You should see:
     - Your wallet address (starts with `G`)
     - Your XLM balance (currently 0)
     - Network selector (ensure you're on correct network)

### Security Best Practices

- **Never share your recovery phrase** - anyone with it has access to all your funds
- **Use a strong, unique password** - different from all your other passwords
- **Test wallet recovery** - verify your recovery phrase works before relying on it
- **Keep your recovery phrase offline** - write it down or store in a password manager
- **Use browser protection** - ensure your computer has antivirus/security software

## Step 2: Fund Your Wallet with XLM

You need XLM (Stellar Lumens) to list and eventually transfer funds from sales. Here's how to get it.

### For Testing (Testnet)

If you're testing PromptHash on testnet:

1. Go to [Stellar Development Foundation Testnet Faucet](https://stellar.org/developers/reference/testnet)
2. Enter your wallet address (from Freighter)
3. Click "Get Started with Test Network"
4. You'll instantly receive test XLM

**Note:** Test XLM has no real value; it's only for development/testing.

### For Real Use (Mainnet)

You need to buy real XLM from an exchange:

**Beginner-Friendly Options:**
1. **Coinbase**
   - Visit https://coinbase.com
   - Create account, verify identity
   - Buy XLM using credit card or bank transfer
   - Withdraw XLM to your Freighter wallet address

2. **Kraken**
   - Visit https://kraken.com
   - Create account, verify identity
   - Trade USD/EUR for XLM
   - Withdraw XLM to your Freighter wallet address

3. **Uphold**
   - Visit https://uphold.com
   - Fast, minimal KYC
   - Buy XLM directly
   - Withdraw to Freighter

**Cost Estimate:**
- Start with 5-10 XLM ($0.50-$1.00 depending on price)
- Covers: listing costs + transaction fees + testing

**Withdrawal Steps:**
1. Copy your Freighter wallet address
2. In exchange: Go to "Withdraw" or "Send"
3. Paste your wallet address
4. Enter amount (e.g., 5 XLM)
5. Confirm
6. Wait 5-15 minutes for deposit to Freighter

### Checking Your Balance

- Open Freighter extension
- Click on your account
- You should see your XLM balance
- Green checkmark means funds are confirmed

## Step 3: List Your First Prompt

### Before You List

Have these ready:
1. **Prompt Title:** (e.g., "ChatGPT Prompt for SEO Blog Outlines")
2. **Category:** (e.g., "Writing", "Marketing", "Code", "Business")
3. **Preview Text:** 2-3 sentences describing what the buyer gets (see [Best Practices](#best-practices-for-previews))
4. **Full Prompt:** Your complete, working prompt
5. **Price:** In XLM (e.g., 0.50 XLM, 1 XLM, 10 XLM - see [Pricing Guide](#pricing-your-prompts))
6. **Featured Image:** (optional) A nice image representing your prompt (PNG/JPG)

### Step-by-Step Listing

1. **Go to PromptHash and Connect Wallet:**
   - Visit the PromptHash Stellar marketplace
   - Click "Connect Wallet" (top-right)
   - Freighter popup appears
   - Click "Approve" to connect

2. **Navigate to "Sell":**
   - Click "Sell" in the main navigation
   - You'll see a "Create New Prompt" form

3. **Fill in Prompt Details:**
   - **Title:** Make it clear and specific
   - **Category:** Choose the closest match
   - **Preview:** Write a compelling preview (see below for tips)
   - **Full Prompt:** Paste your complete prompt here
   - **Price:** Enter price in XLM (e.g., 1.5)
   - **Image URL:** (optional) Link to an image or upload

4. **Review and Submit:**
   - Double-check all information
   - Click "Create Prompt"
   - Freighter popup: Review transaction details
   - Click "Approve" to confirm

5. **Wait for Confirmation:**
   - Transaction sends to blockchain
   - Wait 5-10 seconds for confirmation
   - You'll see: "Prompt listed successfully!"
   - Your prompt is now live on the marketplace

### Congratulations! 🎉

Your prompt is now publicly visible. Buyers can see it in the marketplace and purchase access.

## Best Practices for Previews

Your preview is crucial—it decides if a buyer purchases or scrolls past.

### The Psychology of Conversions

**What buyers are thinking:**
- "Is this prompt actually useful for me?"
- "Will it save me time/money?"
- "Can I use it right away?"
- "Is the price fair?"

**Your preview must answer all of these in 2-3 sentences.**

### Preview Formula

```
[Direct Benefit] + [Specific Use Case] + [Result]
```

**Examples:**

❌ **Bad Preview:**
> "ChatGPT prompt for writing"

✅ **Good Preview:**
> "Use this prompt to generate high-converting product descriptions for Shopify in seconds. Include your product details, and get 3 optimized descriptions ready to paste into your store. Saves hours on copywriting."

---

❌ **Bad Preview:**
> "Coding helper prompt"

✅ **Good Preview:**
> "Generate clean, production-ready Python functions with full docstrings and error handling. Paste your requirements, get working code with tests. Perfect for speeding up backend development."

---

### Preview Checklist

- [ ] Starts with the main benefit (not the tool name)
- [ ] Includes specific use case (not generic)
- [ ] Mentions the output the buyer gets
- [ ] Shows time/effort savings
- [ ] Includes action verbs (Generate, Create, Write, Automate, etc.)
- [ ] Is honest (don't oversell)
- [ ] Fits in 2-3 sentences

### Naming Best Practices

**Good Titles:**
- "LinkedIn Post Generator - Viral Engagement Formula"
- "Code Review Prompt - Find Performance Bugs Fast"
- "Email Subject Line Optimizer - Proven Open Rate Boost"

**Weak Titles:**
- "ChatGPT Prompt" (too generic)
- "Helper" (unclear)
- "Cool Tool" (no benefit)

## Pricing Your Prompts

### Pricing Framework

**Consider these factors:**

1. **Time Saved for Buyer**
   - If it saves 1 hour of work, price high ($1-5 XLM)
   - If it saves 10 minutes, price low ($0.10-0.50 XLM)
   - Formula: Hourly rate × hours saved / 10

2. **Market Competition**
   - Research similar prompts in your category
   - You can undercut slightly to gain initial reviews
   - Or charge premium if yours is clearly better

3. **Perceived Value**
   - High-value use cases (revenue-generating): $1-10 XLM+
   - Productivity helpers (time-saving): $0.50-2 XLM
   - Learning/hobby prompts: $0.10-0.50 XLM

4. **Your Experience Level**
   - Beginner: Start low ($0.25-0.50 XLM) to build reputation
   - Intermediate: $0.50-2 XLM
   - Expert: $2-10+ XLM (especially if niche)

### Pricing Examples

| Prompt Type | Use Case | Suggested Price |
|-------------|----------|-----------------|
| Blog outline generator | Saves 30 mins per article | 0.50-1 XLM |
| Code snippet complete | Saves 1-2 hours | 1-2 XLM |
| Sales email template | Proven high conversion | 2-5 XLM |
| Learning guide starter | Educational | 0.10-0.25 XLM |
| Custom workflow | Specific niche + proven ROI | 5-20 XLM |

### Dynamic Pricing Strategy

**Month 1:** Start at lower price to gain reviews
- Example: 0.50 XLM for a prompt you'd eventually price at 2 XLM
- Why: Reviews build trust; first 5-10 sales are crucial

**After 10 Sales:** Raise price gradually
- Increase by 0.25-0.50 XLM every 10 sales
- Monitor if sales slow down (sign that price is too high)
- Adjust accordingly

**Track Your Data:**
- How many views per day?
- Conversion rate (views → purchases)?
- Are sales slowing down? Price might be too high.

## Marketing and Discovery

Your listing is live, but how do buyers find it?

### Built-In Discovery

1. **Search:** Buyers can search by category, keywords
2. **Featured Prompts:** Top-performing prompts get featured
3. **Creator Profile:** Link to your profile shows all your prompts
4. **Reviews:** Higher rated prompts rank higher

### How to Get Your First Sales

1. **Share Your Profile Link**
   - Go to your creator profile (click your name)
   - Copy the link
   - Share on:
     - Twitter/X (mention #PromptHash #Stellar)
     - LinkedIn (AI/automation community)
     - Discord (AI and crypto communities)
     - Reddit (r/ChatGPT, r/cryptocurrency, niche subreddits)

2. **Ask for Feedback, Not Sales**
   - "Check out my prompt, would love feedback!"
   - Non-pushy, people are more likely to engage
   - Some will buy out of curiosity

3. **Offer Early-Bird Pricing**
   - Price a prompt low for the first week
   - Then increase it
   - Mention: "Early adopters only"

4. **Create a Bundle**
   - If you list multiple prompts, mention them as a collection
   - "3 prompts for X usecase, buy all to maximize value"

5. **Update Your Prompts Regularly**
   - Keep them relevant (update instructions if needed)
   - Remove outdated prompts
   - Show you're an active, engaged creator

## FAQ for Creators

### Q: How do I get paid?

**A:** XLM from sales automatically goes to your wallet. You can:
- Keep it in Freighter (hold for value appreciation)
- Withdraw to an exchange and convert to USD/fiat
- Spend it directly (some places accept XLM)

### Q: What if I want to update my prompt?

**A:** You can edit:
- Title
- Preview text
- Price
- Featured image

You **cannot** change the full encrypted prompt (for buyer security). If you need major changes, create a new listing.

### Q: How do I handle a disputed sale or refund request?

**A:** Per the platform terms:
- Sales are typically final (blockchain immutability)
- For genuine errors, contact platform support
- They may mediate or escalate

### Q: Can I sell the same prompt on other platforms?

**A:** Yes! You can:
- Sell on PromptHash AND other marketplaces
- There are no exclusivity requirements
- Multi-channel approach maximizes your revenue

### Q: How much does PromptHash take as a cut?

**A:** Check the current platform fees in the docs. Typically:
- Creator gets: 95% of sale price
- Platform takes: 5% (to cover infrastructure)
- All transactions are visible on-chain

### Q: What if my prompt isn't selling?

**Troubleshooting:**
1. Improve your preview (see [Best Practices](#best-practices-for-previews))
2. Lower your price (test market demand)
3. Add better category/tags
4. Promote on social media
5. Check if prompt is working as described

### Q: Can I hire someone to write prompts for me?

**A:** Yes, but:
- You take responsibility for quality
- Ensure the creator/freelancer is credited in the preview
- Be transparent about authorship
- Respect intellectual property rights

### Q: What file formats for images?

**A:** Use PNG or JPG, ideally:
- 500x500px or larger
- Under 5MB
- Clear, professional-looking
- Represents your prompt well

### Q: How do I handle competitors copying my prompts?

**A:** They cannot because:
- Your prompt is encrypted end-to-end
- Only buyers with wallet verification access it
- Code/content theft is nearly impossible

What they *might* do:
- Copy your preview/title
- Create similar prompts

For identical copying: Report to platform moderators.

### Q: Can I sell editing/writing services alongside prompts?

**A:** Check platform guidelines. PromptHash focuses on:
- Selling prompts (primary)
- Individual services outside platform (your choice)

Avoid directly selling services through PromptHash if not intended.

### Q: What if I want to stop selling?

**A:** You can:
1. Deactivate prompts temporarily (hide from marketplace)
2. Delete prompts permanently
3. Your sales history remains on-chain (for transparency)
4. You can re-list anytime
